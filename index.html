How to Use the Assistant

Speak or type your question, and the assistant will respond. Below are some commands you can use:

Basic Commands:

  • "What is the capital of France?" – Gets an answer from Wikipedia.
  • "Who is Elon Musk?" – Provides information about a person.
  • "pictures of sunset." – Fetches images from Unsplash.
  • "12 + 5 + 10/2?" – Solves basic math problems.
  • "Weather in New York?" – Gets the latest weather updates.
  • "define evaporation?" – Gets the definition of word.
  • "Internet Speed" – Gets the Internet Speed.
  • "if i born on " dd - january(your month) - year" calculate my age " – Gets the age in years.
  • "If my weight is 30, then what is my approxiamate height?" – Gets the height.
